Our-Work

(What We DO)

What we do in Umvelingangi we teach children about their rights. We run campaigns where children from disadvantaged families comes and get parcel that includes toiletries and food. We host short courses about robotics, artificial, art etc. We are teaching music like playing the drums and piano, we host events where there are different sports. We have the netball court and soccer field where young children come and practice and stay away from drugs. We have a church we educate people about the word of God and give the community hope. We give scholarship and bursaries to children in our community to further their studies. We teach old ages that never went to school so that they can be literate and advance their learning because they have been unable to read. We have skill training that will help you get started in building your business. We host Gender Based Violence and teach youth about it. We provide water for nearest resides that have shortage of water. We have Childline where children report from if they are abused. We encourage learning the agricultural skill as a way of creating entrepreneurial opportunities in the agricultural market.
