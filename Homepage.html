Welcome to Umvelingangi Safe Space and Youth Development Centre NPO organization

Youth need safe public spaces where they can come together, engage in activities related to their diverse needs and interests, participate in decision making processes and freely express themselves.

NPO

The reason for NPO is to provide benefits to the society and give those who lost hope a chance to regain hope. NPOs have separate entity status, meaning they have their own existence, can enter contracts, and are responsible for their obligations. Additionally, NPOs can provide assets or income to individuals only as fair compensation.

NPO

NPOs cannot be political, which is why many of them maintain a non-partisan tone in their communication. They have tax-exempt status granted by the IRS due to their contribution to a social cause and public benefit.
