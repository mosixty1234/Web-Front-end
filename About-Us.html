ABOUT-US

We are located in Winterveld,we assist in the community and we want to build better environment.The aim is to push our youth to think more outside the box and build the positive mindset to our upcoming generation.We work on destroying the negative things that are happening in our community.We want the upcoming generation to have a brighter future that is not influence by people with no good intention in the society.We want to develop software developers,doctors,teachers,pilots and teach everyone about taking life decisions because most youth take wrong path so the Umvelingangi Safe Space is meant to guide and protect the community.

HISTORY

Umvelingangi Safe Space and Youth development Centre was founded in 2019. It has help community in protecting children against bad influences of the street.It has brought change ever since it was develop,and it has created more jobs for the community.

Mission

The mission of the Umvelingangi Safe Space and Youth Development is to see change in the community of Winterveld and Mabopane. The mission is to develop the community of Winterveld and influence Youth to think different.The mission of the organisation is to protect children and to expand their opportunities to reach their full potential and educate them about life.Improve communities we priorities everyone right and teach children about their rights.

Vision

Our organisation vision is to see the crime rate of winterveld decrease and influece other areas to work on protecting South Africa. Our vision is to see every child having the positive impact in their lives and changing their homes background.Our vision is to see our community moving forward and everyone having opportunity.
